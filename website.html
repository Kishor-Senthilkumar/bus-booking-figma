OUR BUSES

We use high quality volvo buses to ensure safety.The multi-axle B11R range consists of the longest buses in India. The 14.5m seater ensures comfortable and safe journeys for up to 55 passengers. The newly introduced completely built sleeper coach can accommodate 40 berths and provide for 12.5 cu.m of personal luggage space. The integration of the B11R chassis with a specifically developed body results in unmatched stability, safety, and reliability. Customers have the advantage of securing deliveries of the fully built coach at the lowest possible lead time as well as one-stop-shop quality assurance and maintenance support.
